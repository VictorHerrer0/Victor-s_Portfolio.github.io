About Myself

For those who want to know me, My name is Victor Herrero and I am a young professional, who has grown a big interest/passion in the world of Data.

It was right after my first internship as a Business Analyst, when I decided to start working on my hard skills such as SQL, Power BI, Python... with the objective of applying to a Data-related job offer in the near future.

However, I started working in the SaaS industry as a Solution Consultant, where I learned how to deal with clients, meet milestones in project timelines and most importantly, where I understood the need of developing a more critical thinking. It also gave me a broader understanding of how Data needs to be processed and the impact that this currently has on decision making at a management level.

This portfolio is a representation of the data analysis skills I have worked on for the past few years.

Enjoy!
